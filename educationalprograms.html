Chess Fundamentals: Mastering the Basics with Naihati New Life Society

The "Chess Fundamentals: Mastering the Basics with Naihati New Life Society" was our first international collaboration with the Naihati New Life Society (NNLS) and facilitated by the DFW Royal Knights. Founded in 2009 by Dr. Subrata Sarkar and a dedicated team, NNLS focuses on empowering underserved communities in the Sundarbans region through education and support. This inaugural event introduced participants to the essentials of chess, including the names and movements of the pieces. It represents the beginning of our international partnership, highlighting our shared commitment to expanding educational opportunities and fostering global community development.
