STEM Spark: Igniting Young Minds in Plano

"STEM Spark: Igniting Young Minds in Plano" was a transformative event that opened the door to the exciting world of STEM for local kids. Led by a member of the Undefined Robotics team, a member of the Plano West team, and a student researching Artificial Intelligence at the University of Dallas, the event offered engaging, hands-on experiences. Participants left inspired, with their curiosity and creativity ignited, paving the way for the next generation of innovators. Click on the Arrows to view the images!
