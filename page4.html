About us

At Solace Global, a Dallas-based nonprofit led by passionate high school students, we are dedicated to making a meaningful impact around the world. Our mission is to ensure that foster homes, senior citizens, and schools locally and  in developing countries such as Pakistan, Somalia, Kenya, and India have access to STEM education through engaging, hands-on experiences. By partnering with robotics and coding nonprofits, we empower communities while also teaching chess and organizing games to foster critical thinking. In addition to providing essential resources like food and shirts, our donation page raises awareness about global issues and enables supporters to contribute to vital causes, allowing donations to be customized and directed where they're needed most. Together, we strive to create a brighter future for all.
